Ebikko is one of the world class cloud-based solution for the enterprise management of electronic documents, records and business processes. It was awarded as the Best of Applications and Infrastructure Tools in the MSC Malaysia APICTA Awards 2010. 


Ebikko's main features are :


  • Able to keep track the whole lifecycle of the document from the creation until the disposition process of the document.

  • Able to be configured for any industry or department.

  • Able to provide information collaboration and sharing capabilities with clear audit trails.

  • Able to be deployed without the need for expensive hardware or software.
